Here's a complete list of the order in which the books should be read for a better enjoyment and understanding of the amazing universe created by our favorite writer, Rick Riordan.

Percy Jackson and the Olympians

  • The Lightning Thief (2005)
  • The Sea of Monsters (2006)
  • The Titan's Curse (2007)
  • The Battle of the Labyrinth (2008)
  • The Last Olympian (2009)

Percy Jackson and the heroes of Olympus

  • The Lost Hero (2010)
  • The Son of Neptune (2011)
  • The Mark of Athena (2012)
  • The House of Hades (2013)
  • The Blood of Olympus (2014)

Companion Books

  • Demigods and monsters (2008)
  • The demigod files (2009)
  • The ultimate guide (2010)
  • Percy Jackson's greek gods (2014)
  • Demigods of Olympus (2015)
  • Percy Jackson's greek heroes (2015)
  • Camp Half-blood confidential (2017)
